**Emacs** was originally written by **Richard Stallman**.

He created the first version in **1976** while working at the **MIT AI Lab**. Later, Stallman rewrote it almost entirely as **GNU Emacs** (released in **1984**), which became a flagship project of the **GNU Project** and a cornerstone of the free software movement.
